Sean's Opinion on Potbelly Sandwich Shop

Potbelly Sandwich Shop

Potbelly is one of the best fast food sandwich shop

Unlike Subway Potbelly doesn’t advertise themselves as a “create your own” sandwich place. Potbelly tries to take some of the creativity out of it by giving you a limited selection of your meat and cheese. Your creativity comes into play after the sandwich has been toasted

Its better than Subway for the same price
